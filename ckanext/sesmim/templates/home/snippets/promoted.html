СЕСМИМ мэдлэгийн порталд тавтай морил

Монгол Улсын олборлох салбарын менежментийг бэхжүүлэх “СЕСМИМ” төслийн Мэдээллийн порталд тавтай морилно уу! Welcome to SESMIM knowledge portal

Welcome to the Strengthening Extractive Sector Management in Mongolia (SESMIM) Project’s Knowledge Portal! SESMIM is funded by the Global Affairs Canada, and implements activities in the fields of strategic revenue management, mine licensing, mine closure, environmental management, cadastre and gender. Materials, hand-outs, manuals and presentations developed for trainings and seminars organized by the project are now publicly available through this portal.

This MERIT Knowledge Portal publishes all training and consulting documents, which are prepared by the long term and short term advisors during the project implementation stage in Mongolia
